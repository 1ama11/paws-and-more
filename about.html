Our Story

Paws & More started in 2019 when our founder, Sara, struggled to find quality, affordable products for her two cats and rescue dog — all in one place. Every pet store was either too expensive, too limited, or had no adoption options at all.

So she built what she wished existed. A cosy, trustworthy place where pet owners could shop for everything their cats and dogs need, and where animals in need could find new families.

Today, Paws & More has helped over 800 animals find loving homes and serves thousands of pet owners every month. We're proud of every tail wag and every purr that followed.
